Scrambled Eggs with Spinach
American Style

A delicious American recipe

10
Minutes
3
Servings
180
Calories
★☆☆
Difficulty

Ingredients

Servings: 3

  • 6large eggs
  • 2cups fresh spinach
  • 2tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 1/4tsp black pepper

Preparation

1
Step 1
Heat olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
2
Step 2
Add spinach and sauté for 1-2 minutes until wilted.
3
Step 3
Crack eggs into a bowl, whisk with salt and pepper.
4
Step 4
Pour eggs into the pan with spinach.
5
Step 5
Gently stir with a spatula until eggs are softly set.
6
Step 6
Remove from heat while slightly underdone (they will continue cooking).
7
Step 7
Serve immediately.

Chef’s Secrets

  • Don't overcook - eggs continue cooking after removed from heat
  • Use fresh spinach for best texture.
